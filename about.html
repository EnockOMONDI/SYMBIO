Who We Are

Providing
Best Healthcare Service Since
2011 to present

Symbio Healthcare Limited is a private limited Company registered in Kenya, with core mandate in warehousing and distribution of pharmaceuticals products. At inception the role was to manage the marketing activities for Denk Pharma and grow the brand within the medical fraternity in Kenya.
Denk Pharma is a German Company, incorporated in Munich in 1948. Other than in Germany, Denk Pharma distributes its products in 65 countries globally. Denk Pharma are manufacturer of premium medicine and food supplement.
Following the huge success Symbio Healthcare was appointed an importer and distributor of Denk Pharma products to serve the Kenyan market. Ever since then we have continued to supply medical drugs in Kenya to range of sectors/clients to building our brand name “SYMBIO”.
